What budget app works for biweekly paychecks?

Biweekly paychecks create a strange problem: bills don’t care about your payday schedule. They come when they come.

If your car payment hits 3 days before your next paycheck, you’re short—even if the rest of the month looks fine.

That’s why monthly budgeting apps fail biweekly workers. You need a week-by-week view that shows:

  • when bills land
  • when paychecks land
  • whether you’ll run out before the next check

FortuneTell shows exactly how many days your money will last and which weeks are tight—so you can plan cashflow around timing, not categories.
