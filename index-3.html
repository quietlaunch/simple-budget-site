FAQ

Questions about FortuneTell.

Does FortuneTell work if my income changes every week?

Yes. FortuneTell assumes irregular income by default. You can enter one-off payments, recurring gigs, retainers, and invoices whenever they're booked, not just when they pay.

Do I have to connect my bank?

No. You can run FortuneTell entirely on manual entry if you prefer. Bank sync, when available, will be optional and clearly labeled. You stay in control.

What's the difference between Free and Essential?

Free gives you a 30-day forecast for one account using local storage. Essential unlocks a 90-day forecast, up to three accounts, sync across devices, summaries, and no ads.

Is my data private?

On the Free plan, your data stays in your browser and never leaves your device unless you export it. When cloud sync arrives on paid plans, it will be clearly explained and optional.
