FAQ

Questions about FortuneTell.

Does FortuneTell work if my income changes every week?

Yes. FortuneTell assumes irregular income by default. You can enter one-off payments, recurring gigs, retainers, and invoices whenever they're planned, not just when they pay.

Do I have to connect my bank?

No. FortuneTell is built for manual entry. No bank connection is required.

What's the difference between Free and Essential?

Free gives you a 30-day forecast for one account using local storage. Essential unlocks a 90-day forecast, up to three accounts, sync across devices, category summaries, and no ads.

Is my data private?

Your budget data is stored locally in your browser. Your account email is stored securely for login. You can export and import JSON backups at any time.
