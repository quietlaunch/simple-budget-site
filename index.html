Will my data be sold or tracked?

No. We don't sell your data, run ads, or turn you into a product. Manual entry keeps you in full control, and optional bank sync only brings in what you choose. Your financial data is yours.

Is this budgeting app really simple to use?

Yes. FortuneTell focuses on the question that matters most: how long will my money last? No complex category trees, no complicated dashboardsâ€”just your cash, your timeline, and clear next steps.

Who is this simple budgeting app for?

FortuneTell is designed for freelancers, gig workers, and solopreneurs with irregular income. If your pay changes week-to-week, month-to-month, or season-to-season, this app is built to match your reality.

Do I have to connect my bank account?

No. You can use manual entry only, or turn on optional sync later if you want automation. Many people prefer to start manual for awareness and then selectively sync once they trust the numbers.

Yes. You can treat a category like a savings bucket that accumulates instead of resetting. Add to it regularly, watch it grow alongside your spending, and use it as a buffer to smooth out slow weeks or irregular invoices.

Do I have to budget monthly?
